ORANGE AD
“جاري يا جاري"

Date : June ,2018 Concept : This AD was for the telecom company ,Orange . Since this was specifically targeted for Egypt and the MENA region during the month of Ramadan the goal was to highlight the feelings of belonging ,coming together, and inclusivity that the eastern culture has merged with the traditions of Ramadan . Features: Donia samir Ghanem,Dhaffer Abdeen,Hend Sabry,Nicole Saba,and Mohamed Mounir Script: the AD portrays friendly and fun interactions between neighbours in an apartment building ,each of them complementing the other while singing a catchy song .
